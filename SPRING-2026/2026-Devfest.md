<summary><h2> FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) </h2></summary>

- **Do I need prior hackathon experience?**

No. This hackathon is designed to be welcoming for first-time participants as well as experienced builders.

- **Can I join without a team?**

Yes. You can join solo and form a team at the event. Teams can have 1 to 4 people max.

- **How will projects be evaluated?**

Projects will be evaluated based on brand fidelity, data precision, outreach effectiveness, scalability, and overall innovation. A fully coded product is not required, but teams should present a strong prototype or demo that clearly shows how the solution works.

Welcome to **DevFest + AI Hackathon — Spring 2026**.

This is a high-impact, one-day hackathon focused on building AI-powered solutions for real-world business challenges.

Participants will design intelligent systems that combine AI, automation, and product thinking to solve scaling problems in luxury markets, content generation, and business development.

No prior hackathon experience required — beginners are welcome.
