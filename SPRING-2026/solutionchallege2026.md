# Google Developer Groups at FIU (GDG at FIU)  
## Solution Challenge 2026 — Info Sessions, Study Jams & Hackathon Prep

*Google Developer Groups at FIU (GDG at FIU) invites students from **all majors** and **all experience levels** to participate in the **Google Solution Challenge 2026**.*

The **Solution Challenge** is a **global Google competition** where student teams identify a **real-world problem** and build an **innovative solution using Google technologies**.  
**No prior experience required** — both **technical and non-technical students** are encouraged to participate.

---

## 🌍 What Is the Solution Challenge?
Student teams will:
- Identify a **local, real-world problem**
- Partner with a **local nonprofit, small business, or community organization**
- Design and build a **technical solution using Google tools**
- Demonstrate **impact, feasibility, and innovation**

Solutions may use tools such as **Gemini API, Firebase, Vertex AI, BigQuery, Google Cloud Platform**, and more.

---

## 📘 What We Cover in Info Sessions
- Overview of the Solution Challenge & judging criteria  
- Eligibility rules and key deadlines  
- Team formation (technical & non-technical roles)  
- Problem discovery & project ideation  
- How to find and work with a **local partner organization**  
- Submission requirements & demo expectations  
- Available resources, mentorship, and support from **GDG at FIU**

**Info sessions run weekly from February 18 to April 11, 2026.**

---

## 👥 Team & Eligibility Requirements
- Teams of **2–4 participants**
- Open to **all majors** and **all universities**
- High school students **(16+)** are welcome
- Teams must partner with a **local business, nonprofit, or community organization**
- Projects must solve a **real, clearly defined problem**
- One submission per team
- **Submission forms are provided to teams that participate in one of our monthly hackathons**

---

## 🗓 Key Dates (2026)
- **Solution Challenge Launch:** February 17  
- **Initial Submission Window:** February 17 – April 20  
- **Submission Deadline:** **April 20, 2026 (PT)**  
- **Preliminary Judging:** April 23 – May 7  
- **Finalist Mentorship:** May 18 – June 8  
- **Regional Demo Day:** July 10, 2026
